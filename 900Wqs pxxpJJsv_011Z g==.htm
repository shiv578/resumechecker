Job ProfilePosition:
** Hackathon for B.Tech CSE Students 
** Case Study for MBA Students.

Job Profile:
** Centific Premier Hackathon 2.0, a 14-day residential innovation challenge, along with aone-week Case Study Competition, to be hosted at our Raheja Mindspace office-Hyderabad.
** This immersive program is designed to provide students with hands-on exposure to cutting-edge technologies, including AI and advanced digital tools. 
** Participants will have the opportunity to work on real-world industry problems, gaining practical insights into AI-driven product development, product management, and workforce operations. 
** The initiative aims to bridge the gap between academia and industry by enabling students to collaborate directly with experienced professionals and AI practitioners.
** Students who successfully complete the full duration of the hackathon and case study challenge will be evaluated for internship opportunities with a Pre-Placement Offer (PPO), accompanied by a competitive industry package.
** Please note that this is a highly intensive and commitment-driven program. 
** Attendance throughout the entire 14-day duration is mandatory, and any absence during the hackathon or case study competition will result in disqualification from further consideration.

Eligibility Criteria:
** For B.Tech CSE:  70 percent in the Current Degree 
** For MBA:  6 CGPA (All Streams ) in the Current Degree
